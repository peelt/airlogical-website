~ what's inside

Complete aircraft management

Eight connected modules, one shared source of truth — so the office and the cockpit always see the same picture.

Aircraft

The aircraft record — particulars, airworthiness, key documents, and the dashboard your team opens first.

Crew

Profiles, licences and ratings, medicals, recency, documents, and per-aircraft permissions.

Certificates

Aircraft, crew and company certificates in one register, with expiry tracking and reminders.

Operations

Every trip end to end — planning, dispatch, handling, permits, and a printable trip pack.

Technical

Maintenance log, life-limited components, the Airworthiness Directive register and open defects.

Safety

An SMS crew actually use — reports, occurrences, flight-ops plans and corrective actions.

Finance

Standing costs, per-leg costs, budgets, pre-trip estimates and multi-currency owner statements.

Compliance

A live forecast of everything coming due across every aircraft and crew member.

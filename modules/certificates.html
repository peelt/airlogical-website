~ certificates

Aircraft & Crew Certificate Tracking Software

Airlogical puts aircraft, crew and company certificates in one register with automatic expiry tracking, document storage and reminders — so nothing lapses by surprise.

Try NowPricing

Most operators don't lose control of certificates through carelessness — they lose it through fragmentation. Aircraft paperwork sits with the technical team, crew licences with the chief pilot, company approvals with the office, and no single view shows what expires next month.

Airlogical consolidates all three into one register. Every certificate carries its issue and expiry dates and the document itself, and everything that's approaching expiry rises to the top of a single ranked list.

~ capabilities

What the Certificates module covers

One register, three scopes

Aircraft, crew and company certificates in a single structure, so nothing depends on remembering which system holds what.

Configurable certificate types

Define the certificate types your operation actually holds, organised into sections, rather than being forced into a fixed list.

Automatic expiry tracking

Every certificate with a date is monitored and surfaced as due-soon or overdue without anyone running a report.

Document storage

The certificate PDF or scan is held against the record, so evidence is one click away during an audit.

Reminders

Upcoming expiries are pushed to the people responsible rather than waiting to be discovered.

Audit-ready views

Filter to what's expired, expiring or complete, and export the picture an inspector asks for.

~ questions

Questions about Certificates

What certificates can Airlogical track?

Aircraft, crew and company certificates alike — from Certificates of Registration, ARCs and insurance through to crew licences, ratings and medicals — all in one register with expiry tracking.

Does it send expiry reminders?

Yes. Certificates approaching expiry are surfaced in the compliance forecast and in reminders, so they can be renewed before they lapse rather than after.

Can I define my own certificate types?

Yes. Certificate types and the sections they sit in are configurable, so the register reflects the certificates your operation actually holds.

Are the actual documents stored?

Yes. The scan or PDF is stored against the certificate record, so the evidence is available immediately during an audit or oversight inspection.

What happens when a certificate is renewed?

You update the record with the new dates and document; the previous version is retained so the history remains auditable.

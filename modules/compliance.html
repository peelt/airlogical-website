~ compliance

Aviation Compliance Software & Live Forecast

Airlogical gives you a single live forecast of everything coming due across every aircraft and crew member — certificates, checks, maintenance and training — ranked by what needs attention first.

Try NowPricing

Compliance rarely fails because nobody knew the rule. It fails because the expiry was in a different system from the person who could act on it, and nobody looked in the right week.

The Compliance module is Airlogical's answer: one forecast that reads from every other module — aircraft airworthiness, crew qualifications and recency, certificates, maintenance and training — and presents it as a single ranked list of what is overdue and what is due soon.

~ capabilities

What the Compliance module covers

One forecast, whole fleet

Every aircraft and every crew member in one view, rather than a report per module.

Ranked by urgency

Overdue first, then due-soon, so the list is a work queue rather than a data dump.

Sourced from the live record

The forecast reads the underlying records, so it can't drift out of date the way a manually maintained tracker does.

Permission-aware

People see the aircraft and sections they're entitled to see, so the forecast is useful without over-sharing.

Surfaced where decisions happen

The same compliance signals appear on the fleet schedule and in each leg's readiness check.

Exportable

Take the position out as a report when an owner, auditor or authority asks for it.

~ questions

Questions about Compliance

What does the compliance forecast show?

Everything coming due — certificate expiries, crew qualifications and recency, maintenance and training — across the whole fleet in one ranked list, so nothing expires by surprise.

Where does the forecast get its data?

From the live records in the other modules. Because it derives rather than duplicates, it cannot drift out of date the way a separate spreadsheet tracker does.

Is it useful for audits?

Yes. The forecast plus the underlying registers give an auditor a current picture and an exportable history of what happened and when.

Does everyone see everything?

No. The forecast respects each person's permissions, so people see the aircraft and sections they're entitled to.

Does compliance affect dispatch?

Yes. The same signals feed the fleet schedule and the pre-departure readiness check, so a compliance problem is visible at the point a leg is planned, not just in a report.
